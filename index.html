Operating System (106F14A)

Xianliang Jiang, FEECS of Ningbo University, Fall Semester 2018.

Overview

The purpose of this course is to teach the design and concepts of operating systems. Topics will cover the concepts of operating system structure, process, thread, process synchronization, CPU scheduling, physical and virtual memory, mass storage, file system, Input/output systems, etc.
